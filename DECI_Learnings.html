My DECI Learning Experience

Topics I Enjoyed the Most

During my DECI learning journey, I particularly enjoyed working with:

  • Thunkable - Creating mobile apps visually was so much fun!
  • Blockly - The block-based programming made concepts so clear.
  • Multimedia - Working with different media formats was creative and engaging.
These topics were enjoyable because they allowed me to create tangible projects while learning.

I'm excited to deepen my knowledge in:

  • Website Development - Learning how to create complete websites from scratch.
  • Encoding - Understanding how data is represented and transformed.
These areas seem essential for my future digital projects and career development.
